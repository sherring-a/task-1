JOURNAL 3

This is a mysterious journal that was discovered in the woods, seeming to contain all the secrets of Gravity Falls...

This mysterious journal was discovered in the woods by 12 year old Dipper Pines. This journal has been seen to hold mysteries and secrets within our mysterious town of Gravity Falls! Though the boy refuses to hand it over to anyone. Witnesses who have had glances at the pages of the book say that the book contains things such as demon summoning and worship, mysterious doors, other dimensions and beasts that roam our forests and lakes! If anybody has any information regarding this journal please let the local police Blubs and Durland know!
